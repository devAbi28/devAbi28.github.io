Second Chance Aspin Shelter Philippines Inc.

Established in 2018, Second Chance Aspin Shelter Philippines Incorporated provided a home for strays, abused, and abandoned cats and dogs. Aspin or "Asong Pinoy" (directly translated as "Filipino Dogs") are Philippine native dogs, while Puspins ("Pusang Pinoy," directly translated as "Filipino Cats"). We strive to give them a second chance by providing them a home and preparing them for their furrever homes. Unfortunately, strays are rampant in the Philippines, and there are many cases of abandonment and animal cruelty. The shelter is currently located at Calamba, Laguna. Originally, SECASPI is a home for 40+ dogs and 20+ cats. We currently reached our limit of 50+ dogs because of animals being dumped in front of the shelter. We are still hoping to find a forever home for more than half of them. The costs of treating, rehabilitating, and giving these animals a happy life are solely funded by donations.

Our Services

Services That We Offer

Spay and Neuter

Our Spay and Neuter program is dedicated to controlling the pet population and improving animal health. By providing free spaying and neutering services, we help prevent unwanted litters, reduce the number of homeless animals, and promote longer, healthier lives for pets. Support our initiative to create a more sustainable and compassionate community for all animals.

Stray Animals Feeding Program

Our program aims to improve their quality of life and foster a healthier, more compassionate environment for all animals. Join us in our mission to care for these vulnerable creatures and make a tangible difference in their lives.

Free Vaccinations

Our Free Vaccinations program ensures that all animals at the shelter receive essential immunizations at no cost. By providing vital vaccines, we protect our pets from common diseases, promote their long-term health, and prepare them for adoption into loving homes. Join us in safeguarding the well-being of these animals and giving them a healthy start to their new lives.

Community Engagement

Our Community Engagement program aims to build strong relationships between the animal shelter and the local community. Through educational workshops, volunteer opportunities, and community events, we raise awareness about animal welfare and promote responsible pet ownership. By involving community members in our mission, we create a supportive network dedicated to improving the lives of animals.

Donation Drive

Our Donation Drive is crucial for supporting the needs of our animal shelter. By collecting funds, food, supplies, and other essentials, we ensure that every animal receives the care and attention they deserve. Your generous contributions help provide medical treatment, nutritious meals, and a safe, comfortable environment for our pets. Join us in making a difference—every donation brings us one step closer to finding forever homes for these loving animals.

Shelter Merchandise

Show your support and spread awareness by proudly wearing and using our branded items. Shop with us to make a difference—each item helps fund our mission to provide loving homes and essential services for pets in need.
